Tool Wear Failure (TWF)

When:

tool becomes too worn out

This is like:

brake wear in cars
tire wear
engine wear

Classic maintenance problem.

2. Heat Dissipation Failure (HDF)

Machine overheats because cooling is insufficient.

Condition:

process temp too close to air temp
low RPM

This means:

machine cannot remove heat properly

Very realistic engineering problem.

3. Power Failure (PWF)

Power calculation becomes abnormal.

The dataset uses:

P=τω

Where:

P = power
τ = torque
ω = rotational speed

Too low or too high power causes failure.

This is similar to:

engine overload
unstable motor operation
4. Overstrain Failure (OSF)

Too much combined stress:

high torque
high wear

The machine exceeds safe operating limits.

This is similar to:

pushing machinery too hard
mechanical fatigue
5. Random Failure (RNF)

Tiny random chance of failure.

Represents:

unpredictable events
manufacturing defects
random malfunctions
